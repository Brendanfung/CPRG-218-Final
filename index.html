Brendan Fung Design

Hi, I'm Brendan Fung, a junior designer based in Calgary, Alberta. I specialize in creating unique and memorable visual media content that stands out. My work spans a variety of disciplines, including web design, video production, and graphic design. I always aim to bring fresh ideas and thoughtful execution to every project. I'm currently completing my Graphic Design Diploma at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT), where I’ve gained a solid foundation in graphic, web, and UX/UI design. This program has deepened my understanding of how design can combine form and function to create seamless, user-friendly experiences. I’ve had the tremendous opportunity to work on a wide range of commissioned projects—each one allowing me to explore different aspects of design and expand my creative skillset. I always focus on delivering high-quality results that make an impact. At the core of my approach is the belief that design should be more than just visually appealing—it should serve a purpose. Whether it's enhancing the user experience, telling a compelling story, or bringing a brand’s vision to life, I strive to create designs that are both beautiful and functional. I’m always looking for new challenges and opportunities to push my creativity and continue growing as a designer. Find my contact info below.
